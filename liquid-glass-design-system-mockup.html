01 — Philosophy

Three Pillars

Notion for architecture. Apple for aesthetics. Dark-first, amber-accented, generous with space.

01

Content Elevation

Glass and translucency push content forward, not decorate. Every surface, border, and glow serves hierarchy — making the important things unmissable and the chrome invisible.

02

Warmth Through Restraint

Amber is used surgically — active states, tinted glass, subtle glow. The dominant visual is deep dark with luminous punctuation, not an amber wash.

03

Breathing Room

Generous negative space between elements. Cards float, don't stack. Text has room to read. The app should feel like it has less on screen than it actually does.
